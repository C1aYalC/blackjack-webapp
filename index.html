Objective:

Beat the dealer by getting as close to 21 as possible without going over.

Card Values:

  • Number cards (2-10): Face value
  • Face cards (J, Q, K): Worth 10
  • Ace: Worth 1 or 11 (whichever is better)

How to Play:

  • Place your bet (minimum 500 Chips)
  • You and the dealer each get 2 cards
  • One of the dealer's cards is hidden
  • Hit: Take another card
  • Stand: Keep your current hand
  • Double Down: Double your bet and take one final card

Winning:

  • Get closer to 21 than the dealer without busting
  • Dealer must hit on 16 or less, stand on 17 or more
  • Bust (over 21) = automatic loss
  • Blackjack (21 with 2 cards) pays 1.5x your bet
